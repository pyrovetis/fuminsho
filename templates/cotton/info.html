What is fuminshō?

I made fuminshō to solve a personal problem—I've got over 500+ videos saved in a YouTube playlist, and finding what I want was becoming a hassle. So, I built this app to make it easier for myself (and others) to organize and explore playlists.

It's designed to help you quickly sift through your playlists, no matter how big they are. I use it, but feel free to give it a try and enjoy!
